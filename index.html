About me

Hi, my name is Jessica Sea. I'm a front end web developer based out of Toronto, Canada. After years of studying biology and a career in sales, I realized I had a love for learning, taking on new challenges and building relationships. Because of these three things, I was led to a new and exciting hobby, web development! As a recent grad from HackerYou's immersive web development bootcamp, the journey learning to build beautiful and functional websites has sparked a new fire within me. Feel free to message me and we can chat about code (or dogs)!

When I'm not coding, you can find me travelling the globe, hiking to the highest peaks or petting strangers' dogs (with permission, of course).
